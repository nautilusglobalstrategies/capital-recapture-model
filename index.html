Income Deposits

Deposits are modeled by frequency: weekly = every 7 days, bi-weekly = every 14 days, monthly = every 30 days.

Monthly Obligations

Monthly obligations are withdrawn once per month on day 30, after deposits that day. Do not include the primary debt payment here.

Primary Debt

Primary debt interest is modeled monthly using 30-day months. Standard payment is applied on the selected day.
