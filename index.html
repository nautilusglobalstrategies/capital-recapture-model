Capital Recapture Engine™

Capital Recapture Model

A decision tool designed to model how capital can be recaptured, redeployed, and cycled to accelerate debt elimination.

Purpose: compare a standard payoff path against a structured recapture cycle using deposit timing, recurring obligations, and a hidden Level 1 capital layer.

Inputs

Enter your deposits, monthly obligations, and primary debt profile.

Income Deposits

Deposits are modeled by actual date frequency: weekly = every 7 days, bi-weekly = every 14 days, monthly = same day each month when possible.

Monthly Obligations

Monthly obligations are withdrawn on the last day of each month, after deposits that day. Do not include the primary debt payment here.

Primary Debt

The model uses daily accrual and real calendar months. Standard payment is applied on the selected day each month.

What this models

This model compares a standard payoff path against the Capital Recapture Engine™, where capital is repeatedly deployed and recaptured through a structured cycle.

Engine upgrades

Daily Accrual
Both paths accrue interest daily rather than relying on simplified 30-day monthly math.
Real Calendar Dates
Monthly events follow actual calendar months and day counts, improving timing realism.
Interactive Visualization
Charts show how balances and interest paths diverge over time.
